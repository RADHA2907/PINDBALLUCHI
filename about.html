Our Heritage

Where Punjab Meets Agra

Pind Balluchi was born from a passion for authentic Punjabi cooking — the kind that fills a home with aroma, laughter, and warmth. Established over 15 years ago in the historic city of Agra, we set out to bring the bold, hearty flavours of North India's heartland to every guest who walks through our doors.

Our name says it all — 'Pind' (village) and 'Balluchi' (a symbol of Punjabi pride) — together representing our deep roots in the tradition of rustic, soul-warming cuisine. Each dish is cooked using time-honoured recipes passed down through generations, using the finest fresh ingredients.

Located just moments from the iconic Taj Mahal, we are proud to serve both local Agraites and travellers from around the world seeking an unforgettable taste of real North Indian food.
